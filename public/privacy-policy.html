Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Top Level Tutoring (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide when using our website, lead forms, or communication channels.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you submit a form or contact us:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Subject(s) you need tutoring in
  • Grade level
  • Scheduling availability
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

We may also collect basic website analytics such as IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your tutoring inquiry
  • Contact you to schedule sessions
  • Provide and improve our tutoring services
  • Send reminders, follow-ups, or updates you’ve requested or reasonably expect

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. How We Share Information

We may share your information only with:

  • Service providers who help operate our website and tools (for example, hosting or email services)
  • Platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram) in order to deliver and receive your lead form submission

We do not share your data with unrelated advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties for their own marketing.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to help protect your personal information against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to update or correct your information
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Opt out of future communications

To make a request, email us at: arie@topleveltutoring.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

We primarily provide services to middle school, high school, and college students. For younger students, we collect information from parents or legal guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our website or services after any changes means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

Email: arie@topleveltutoring.com
